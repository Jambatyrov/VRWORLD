What is the VRWORLD?

It is based on 2 types of virtual reality glasses that can be used in games, as well as for the development of your business. Up to 10 people can take part in the game at the same time. In the virtual world, all participants see each other's characters and can interact with each other. In business, you can fully immerse yourself in the sphere and feel the whole atmosphere for yourself.
